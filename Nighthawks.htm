Synopsis:

Two N.Y. cops whose regular beat is street crime, get involved in a fight against international terrorists. Tense suspense in a tram car high above Manhattan. An ITA Award winner.

"Nighthawks" was Dutch actor Rutger Hauer's first American film. Additional cast: Walter Matthews (as Commissioner), E. Brian Dean (Sergeant), Caesar Cordova (Puerto Rican Proprietor), Charles Duval (Dr. Ghiselin), Howard Stein (Disco Manager), Tawn Christian (Disco Hostess), and Jamie Gillis (Designer). Filmed in Technicolor.

Tough New York cop Deke Da Silva pursues Europe's most ruthless terrorists through the streets, subways, and nightclubs of Manhattan.
