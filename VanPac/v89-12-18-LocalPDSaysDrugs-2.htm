December 18, 1989, Monday

SECTION: Pg. 1

LENGTH: 35 words

HEADLINE: Judge murdered

BYLINE: By Our New York Staff

BODY:


Police believe that Columbian drug dealers may have sent the postal Bomb which killed an Alabama judge at the weekend. Judge Robert Vance had handled many drug cases. His wife, Helen, was seriously injured.
