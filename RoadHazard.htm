Dettling's garage contained a copy of a road hazard bomb similar to the one that injured Gary Wright. The prominently displayed item in the garage consisted of two 2x4's with a crown of 6" or 8" nails in the middle and a rectangular hole (2"x8") roughly chiseled/carved through the bottom board directly underneath the crown of nails. The device was located on the edge of the large blue tarp covered mound near the door to the kitchen.
