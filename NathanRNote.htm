Expanded NY Times Front Page

Every example of the Nathan R note has now been removed from the internet. A similar fate has befallen the images of the Kaczynski photo and the morphed sketch which "They" used to frame him. Fortunately it was published on the Front Page of the New York Times on Oct 7 1993 and is still widely available on Microfiche in Libraries. Given the extent of efforts surrounding this case and its cover-up, only time will tell the tale of the ultimate fate of all those Microfiche copies of the Image of the Note that started the Million Dollar reward on Oct 7,1993.
