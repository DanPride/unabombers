December 15, 1994, Thursday, FINAL EDITION

SECTION: EDITORIAL, Pg. 20A, On the other hand

LENGTH: 81 words

BYLINE: DAN BERGER

BODY:

It's amazing how computers replicate the human mind. Intel even developed a chip that errs in balancing a checkbook.

The gummint doesn't know whether to catch Unabomber, or turn him against the Enemy.

An immense number of nationalities live in the confined Caucasus mountains. The Appalachians cover a lot more ground and contain only three or four.

Fashion is illusion. The French designer Alix Gres did not let anyone know she had died until a year after she did it.
