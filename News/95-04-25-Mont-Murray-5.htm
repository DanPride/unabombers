The Gazette (Montreal)

April 25, 1995, Tuesday, FINAL EDITION

SECTION: NEWS; NEWSLINE; IN BRIEF; Pg. B1/BREAK

LENGTH: 73 words

HEADLINE: Package bomb kills one

DATELINE: SACRAMENTO, Calif.

BODY:

A package bomb exploded inside the offices of a lobbying group for the wood products industry yesterday, blowing out windows and doors. An employee of the California Forestry Association who opened the package was killed. Police believe it was the work of a serial bomber known as Unabomber, who has been linked to 15 attacks that have left two people dead and 22 injured since 1978, and not to last week's bombing in Oklahoma City.
