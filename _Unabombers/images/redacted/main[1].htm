  Web Hosting News  

New POP and FTP servers
We've installed new POP and FTP servers for our Web Hosting customers. You'll need to change the settings on your mail and ftp programs to use them. The old servers have finally been turned off. Therefore we ask that you please make these changes at your earliest convenience. Your password will not change. Follow this link for answers to Frequently Asked Questions about this.

For POP mail retrieval from your Hosting account, your settings should be: 

     ServerName: pop.business.earthlink.net
     UserName: yourmailboxname%yourdomain.com
Replace yourmailboxname with the name of the mail box you want to check, and yourdomain.com with your domain name.

For FTP access your settings should be: 

     ServerName: ftp.business.earthlink.net
     UserName:   webuserid%yourdomain.com or
     UserName:   ftpdropboxname%yourdomain.com
Replace webuserid and ftpdropboxname with the Web Hosting userid or ftp drop box name you use now.

The procedure for anonymous ftp access will not change.
