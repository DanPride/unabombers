April 27, 1995, Thursday,

SECTION: EDITORIAL, Pg. 14A, On the other hand

LENGTH: 66 words

BYLINE: DAN BERGER

BODY:

Unabomber demands our attention. He resents the distraction of John Doe No. 2. Humor him (or her, or them).

Republicans are rethinking their passion for assault rifles. About time.

Big-mouthed, ignorant, bigoted, abusive, rabble-rousing, hate-mongering know-nothings don't bomb public places. Bombers do.

A special corner of ballroom dance heaven was reserved just for Ginger Rogers.
