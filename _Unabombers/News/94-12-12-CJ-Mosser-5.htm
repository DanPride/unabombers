HEADLINE: BOMBING MAY BE LINKED TO OTHERS

DATELINE: NEWARK, N.J.

BODY:

A bomber who has killed one person and injured 23 others over the last 16 years appears to have struck again, this time killing an advertising executive with a mail bomb, the FBI said yesterday.

Thomas J. Mosser, 50, was killed Saturday in his kitchen in suburban North Caldwell when he opened a small package addressed to him and delivered by the Postal Service, FBI agent Barry Mawn said. The FBI believes Mosser's death is linked to what it code-names the "Unabom" series of mail bombs sent to professors and executives, Mawn said.
